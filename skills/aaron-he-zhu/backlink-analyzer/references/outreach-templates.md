# Outreach Templates

Copy-ready email templates for link building. Personalize every email, lead with value, keep to 100-150 words, one clear ask per email.

---

## General Principles

| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Personalize every email | Reference specific content |
| Lead with value | Their benefit before your ask |
| Keep it short | 100-150 words max |
| One clear ask | Don't combine requests |
| Professional sender | name@yourdomain.com |
| No attachments first email | Triggers spam filters |
| Follow up once or twice | Most responses come from follow-ups |

---

## 1. Broken Link Building

**Subject:** Found a broken link on your [topic] page

Hi [Name],

I was reading your article on [specific article title] -- great resource on [brief compliment].

I noticed the link to [description of broken link] in the [section name] section returns a 404.

I recently published a guide on [your topic] that covers similar ground: [Your URL]

It might work as a replacement. Either way, wanted to give you a heads-up.

Best,
[Your Name]

## 2. Guest Post Pitch

I'd love to contribute a guest post:

**Title:** [Proposed title]
**Angle:** [1-2 sentences on unique angle]
**Why your readers care:** [1 sentence on value]

Writing samples:
- [Sample URL 1]
- [Sample URL 2]

Would this be a fit?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your title / credentials]

## 4. Unlinked Mention

**Subject:** Thanks for mentioning [brand name]

Hi [Name],

Just came across your article [article title] -- thanks for mentioning [your brand]. Really appreciate the kind words about [specific thing they said].

Would you be able to add a link to [Your URL] where you mention us? It would help your readers find us directly.

Totally understand if not possible. Either way, glad you found [product/service] useful.

Best,
[Your Name]

## 7. Skyscraper Technique

**Subject:** Updated resource on [topic]

Hi [Name],

I noticed you linked to [competitor's article] in your post on [their article title].

We just published an updated version: [Your URL]

Improvements over the original:
- [e.g., "2025 data instead of 2022"]
- [e.g., "interactive calculator included"]
- [e.g., "covers 3 additional sub-topics"]

If you think it's a better resource for your readers, I'd appreciate the swap. No worries either way.

Best,
[Your Name]

## Follow-Up Sequence

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Initial | Day 0 | Make the ask |
| Follow-up 1 | Day 5-7 | Gentle reminder |
| Follow-up 2 | Day 14 | Final attempt |
| Stop | After FU2 | Move on |

### Follow-Up Template (Day 5-7)

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

Hi [Name],

Just bumping this in case it got buried. [One-sentence reminder of original ask.]

No worries if the timing isn't right.

[Your Name]

### Final Follow-Up (Day 14)

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

Hi [Name],

Last follow-up on this. [One-sentence reminder.]

Feel free to bookmark [Your URL] for whenever it might be useful.

Best,
[Your Name]

## Response Handling

**They'll add the link:**
> Great, [Name] -- really appreciate it. The exact URL is: [URL]. Preferred anchor text: [text]. Happy to return the favor.

**They want a guest post:**
> Wonderful. Here's what I'm thinking: **Title**: [title], **Outline**: [sections], **Length**: [words], **Delivery**: [date]. Do you have editorial guidelines?

**They're noncommittal:**
> Totally understand. I'll keep [URL] updated. Feel free to reach out if I can be a source or contributor.

**They decline:**
> Completely understand. Thanks for letting me know. If anything changes, don't hesitate to reach out.

_Never argue or send additional follow-ups after a decline._

**No response after full sequence:** Add to "re-engage in 3-6 months" list with a new angle. Never reference prior non-response.

## Subject Line Patterns

| Strategy | Options |
|----------|---------|
| Broken link | "Found a broken link on your [topic] page" / "Quick heads-up about [page title]" |
| Guest post | "Guest post idea: [title]" / "[Topic] contributor for [their site]" |
| Resource page | "Resource for your [topic] page" / "Suggestion for your [topic] list" |
| Unlinked mention | "Thanks for mentioning [brand]" / "Re: your mention of [brand/data]" |
| Digital PR | "New data: [headline stat]" / "Exclusive: [finding]" |
| Skyscraper | "Updated resource on [topic]" / "Better version of [competitor content]" |

**Avoid**: "Link exchange opportunity", "SEO partnership", ALL CAPS, "I'd love a backlink", fake "Re:" subjects, subjects >60 chars.

# Title and Headline Formulas

Proven title structures that drive clicks and rankings. Target 50-60 characters for optimal SERP display.

## Numbered List Formulas

**Pattern**: [Number] [Adjective] [Topic] [Qualifier/Benefit]
- "7 Ways to Improve Your SEO Rankings in 2024"
- "15 Content Marketing Strategies That Actually Work"
- "10 Free Tools for Keyword Research (Beginner-Friendly)"

Best practices: Odd numbers often outperform even; use specific numbers; keep under 30; match to actual content sections.

---

## How-To Formulas

**Pattern 1**: How to [Goal] in [Timeframe]
- "How to Rank on Google in 30 Days"

**Pattern 2**: How to [Goal] Without [Objection]
- "How to Increase Traffic Without Paid Ads"

**Pattern 3**: How to [Goal] (Even If [Limitation])
- "How to Rank #1 (Even If You're a New Website)"

**Pattern 4**: How to [Goal] Like [Authority]
- "How to Do Keyword Research Like an SEO Pro"

## Question Formulas

**What Is**: What Is [Topic]? [Benefit/Hook]
- "What Is Technical SEO? A Complete Guide"

**Why**: Why [Action/State] [Result]
- "Why Most Content Marketing Strategies Fail"

**Should You**: Should You [Action]? [Quick Answer]

## CTR Optimization Patterns

### Elements That Increase CTR
- **Year/Timeframe**: signals freshness
- **Brackets/Parentheticals**: adds context — "(That Actually Work)"
- **Numbers**: promise specific value
- **Power words**: emotional trigger — Ultimate, Essential, Proven
- **Target audience**: relevance — "for Beginners", "for B2B"

### Elements That Decrease CTR
- All caps, excessive punctuation, clickbait, generic titles

## Title Length Guidelines

| Platform | Optimal | Max Before Truncation |
|----------|---------|----------------------|
| Google SERP | 50-60 chars | ~70 chars |
| Google Mobile | 50-55 chars | ~55 chars |
| Twitter/X | 70-100 chars | — |
| Facebook | 40-50 chars | — |
| Email subject | 40-50 chars | 60 chars |

## Title Writing Checklist

- [ ] Includes primary keyword naturally
- [ ] Length is 50-60 characters
- [ ] Matches search intent
- [ ] Contains power word or emotional trigger
- [ ] Specific and concrete
- [ ] Promises clear value
- [ ] Accurate representation of content
- [ ] Unique vs competitors
- [ ] Works when truncated on mobile

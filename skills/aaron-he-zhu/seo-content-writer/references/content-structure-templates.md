# Content Structure Templates

Markdown templates for common SEO content types. Customize [bracketed placeholders] while maintaining structural framework.

## Blog Post (Informational)

**Word count**: 1,200-1,800 | **Goal**: Educate, build authority, rank for informational queries

```markdown
# [Primary Keyword] - [Benefit or Hook]

[Opening hook - 1 compelling sentence]

[Problem statement - pain point reader faces]

[Promise - what readers will learn]

In this guide, you'll discover:
- [Key takeaway 1]
- [Key takeaway 2]
- [Key takeaway 3]

## What Is [Topic]?

[Clear definition in 40-60 words - GEO-optimized]

[Expanded explanation with context]

## Why [Topic] Matters

[Benefit 1 with supporting evidence]

[Benefit 2 with supporting evidence]

> **Key Insight**: [Highlighted important point or statistic]

## [Secondary Keyword or Main Section 1]

[Introduction to this section]

### [Sub-topic 1]

[Detailed explanation]

**Key points**:
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3

### [Sub-topic 2]

[Detailed explanation with example or case study]

## [Secondary Keyword or Main Section 2]

| Factor | Description | Impact |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| [Factor 1] | [Description] | High/Medium/Low |
| [Factor 2] | [Description] | High/Medium/Low |

## [Secondary Keyword or Main Section 3]

**Best practices**:
1. [Practice 1 with explanation]
2. [Practice 2 with explanation]
3. [Practice 3 with explanation]

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### [Mistake 1]
[Why this is a mistake and how to avoid it]

### [Mistake 2]
[Why this is a mistake and how to avoid it]

## Frequently Asked Questions

### [Question from PAA or common query]?
[Direct answer in 40-60 words]

### [Question 2]?
[Direct answer]

## Conclusion

[Recap key points - include primary keyword]

[Clear call-to-action: next step for reader]

---
**Related reading**: [Internal link 1] | [Internal link 2] | [Internal link 3]
```

**Links**: 3-5 internal, 2-3 external authoritative | **Keywords**: Primary in H1, first 100 words, conclusion; secondary in H2s | **GEO**: Definition block, FAQ section, quotable statistics

## Comparison Article ("[A] vs [B]")

```markdown

[Hook - present comparison dilemma and context]

**Quick answer**: [Brief verdict for immediate answer]

| [Feature 3] | [Details] | [Details] |

| **Starting price** | [Price] | [Price] |

## What Is [Option A]?

[Clear definition and key features]

**Pros**: [Advantage 1] | [Advantage 2] | [Advantage 3]

### [Feature Category 1]

**[Option A]**: [Detailed explanation]
**[Option B]**: [Detailed explanation]
**Winner**: [A/B/Tie] - [Brief reasoning]

### [Feature Category 2]

**[Option A]**: [Details]
**[Option B]**: [Details]
**Winner**: [A/B/Tie] - [Brief reasoning]

## Pricing

| Plan | [Option A] | [Option B] |
|------|-----------|-----------|

**Choose [Option A] if**: [Criteria 1] | [Criteria 2] | [Criteria 3]

## Frequently Asked Questions

[Answer with explanation]

[Balanced conclusion with recommendation based on use cases]
```

**Links**: Detailed reviews of each option, related comparisons | **Keywords**: Both options throughout, comparison keywords in H2s

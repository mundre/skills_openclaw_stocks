# Moving Company Video

Help moving companies create trust and service process videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (company overview, packing process, long-distance vs. local service, specialty items, customer testimonial, preparation guide)
2. Gather: service types (local/long-distance/international/specialty), packing services, crew training approach, insurance/claims process, customer communication standards
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest: crew packing demonstration with care, customer describing a stress-free move, coordinator explaining the communication process, before/after truck loading organization, claims process transparency
